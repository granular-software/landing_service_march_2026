3. Purposes and legal bases

The processing carried out through this site mainly serves the following purposes:

  • responding to your requests and discussing a need or project with you;
  • organizing a meeting or other contact with you;
  • ensuring the proper functioning, security, and administration of the site;
  • managing legal obligations and, where applicable, the defense of our rights.

The legal bases relied upon are, depending on the case:

  • the performance of pre-contractual steps taken at your request;
  • our legitimate interest in operating and securing our site and managing professional communications;
  • compliance with our legal obligations;
  • your consent, where required.

We maintain internal documentation of our processing activities where required by applicable law and where useful to support accountability and compliance.

4. Booking a meeting

The meeting booking page on this site includes a scheduling module provided by HubSpot. If you use this functionality, the information you submit is processed in order to organize the meeting, manage the commercial exchange, and follow up on your request.

In that context, HubSpot acts as a technical service provider and may process certain data on our behalf in accordance with its own contractual and privacy documentation.

5. Recipients of the data

Your data is accessible, within the scope of their responsibilities, to authorized persons at Granular and to our technical processors where necessary for the operation of the site, booking, hosting, or security.

We do not sell your personal data.

6. Retention periods

Data is kept for a limited period that is proportionate to the purpose for which it is processed. As a working standard for this site:

  • contact and commercial prospecting data is retained for up to 3 years from the last meaningful contact, unless a longer retention period is required or justified by an ongoing relationship;
  • contractual, billing, or accounting-related data is retained for up to 5 years where necessary for contract management, evidence, or legal defense, without prejudice to longer statutory retention periods where applicable;
  • technical data and security logs are generally retained for up to 12 months, unless a longer period is necessary for incident investigation, security, or legal compliance;
  • data may be archived for longer where required by law or where necessary to establish, exercise, or defend legal claims.

7. Transfers outside the European Union

Some technical service providers may be located outside the European Union or may transfer certain data outside the European Union. In particular, meeting booking on this site relies on HubSpot, which may involve transfers to the United States.

Where this happens, we ensure that such transfers rely on an appropriate GDPR compliance mechanism, such as an adequacy decision, the EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework where applicable, or standard contractual clauses.

8. Security

We implement technical and organizational measures designed to protect personal data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.
